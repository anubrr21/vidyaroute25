Detailed Career Paths

Engineering

B.Tech/B.E. → Specialization → Engineer → Senior Engineer → Project Manager

Average starting salary: ₹6-10 LPA | Growth: High

What Engineering Offers:
  • Diverse specializations: Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, etc.
  • Opportunities in both public and private sectors
  • Global career prospects with high demand
  • Continuous learning and skill development
  • Entrepreneurship opportunities in tech startups

Medical

MBBS → MD/MS → Resident Doctor → Specialist → Senior Doctor

Average starting salary: ₹8-12 LPA | Growth: Very High

What Medical Offers:
  • Prestigious and respected profession
  • Opportunity to save lives and serve society
  • Diverse specializations: Surgery, Pediatrics, Cardiology, etc.
  • Job security and high earning potential
  • Opportunities for research and academia

Commerce

B.Com → MBA/CA → Accountant → Financial Analyst → Finance Manager

Average starting salary: ₹4-7 LPA | Growth: Moderate

What Commerce Offers:
  • Professional courses: CA, CS, CMA
  • Opportunities in banking, finance, and accounting
  • Corporate career paths with growth potential
  • Entrepreneurship opportunities in business
  • Global opportunities in finance sector

Arts & Humanities

B.A. → M.A./B.Ed. → Teacher → Professor/Content Creator → Education Head

Average starting salary: ₹3-5 LPA | Growth: Steady

What Arts & Humanities Offer:
  • Diverse fields: Psychology, Sociology, Literature, History, etc.
  • Opportunities in education, civil services, and media
  • Creative career paths in writing, arts, and content creation
  • Research opportunities in social sciences
  • Roles in NGOs and social work

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about विद्याRoute and our services

How does the career assessment work?

Our career assessment uses a scientifically designed questionnaire to evaluate your interests, skills, personality traits, and values. The AI algorithm then matches your profile with suitable career paths and provides personalized recommendations.

Is विद्याRoute free to use?

Yes, our basic features including career assessment, college search, and career information are completely free. We offer premium services for personalized counseling and detailed reports at affordable prices.

How accurate are the career recommendations?

Our recommendations are based on extensive research and data analysis. While we strive for accuracy, we always recommend using our suggestions as a starting point for further research and discussion with career counselors.

Do you provide counseling services?

Yes, we have a network of experienced career counselors who provide one-on-one guidance sessions. These can be booked through our platform for personalized advice based on your assessment results.
