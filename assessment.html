Assessment FAQs

How long does the assessment take?

The assessment typically takes 10-15 minutes to complete. There are 15 questions, and you can take as much time as you need for each one.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All assessment responses are anonymous and stored securely. We do not share your personal information with third parties.

Can I retake the assessment?

Absolutely! You can retake the assessment as many times as you'd like. Your interests may change over time, so we encourage periodic reassessments.

How accurate are the results?

Our assessment is based on established career counseling principles and has been validated with thousands of students. However, we recommend using the results as a starting point for further exploration rather than a definitive answer.
