Timeline FAQs

How do I customize my timeline?

You can customize your timeline by selecting your target stream (Engineering, Medical, Commerce, etc.) and your current academic year. The timeline will automatically adjust to show relevant milestones and deadlines for your chosen path.

Can I sync the timeline with my calendar?

Yes, you can export important dates and deadlines to your Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or any other calendar app that supports .ics files. This helps you stay organized across all your devices.

How often is the timeline updated?

Our timeline is updated regularly with the latest exam dates, application deadlines, and other important information from official sources. You'll also receive notifications about any changes to your saved timeline.

Can I share my timeline with parents or mentors?

Absolutely! You can generate a shareable link to your timeline or export it as a PDF to share with parents, teachers, or mentors who are supporting your academic journey.
